Billing & plans.

GitHub offers different paid plans 33 . Each of the plans has its own benefits (e.g., more storage, more collaborators) but the GitHub essentials work perfectly fine under the free plan. I would certainly recommend upgrading once you become more familiar with Git, GitHub, and start developing more projects. However, there is no need to pay anything at this point.

Bonus! Students get free GitHub Pro and access to a multitude of extra deals with their Student Developer Pack 34 . Teachers 35  and Schools 36  can also find benefits (some require a payment).
